Slide 1

Stale replay makes fixed clipping brittle

A batch can mix fresh and stale policy ratios. When one ratio dominates, the effective batch shrinks even if the nominal batch size looks healthy.

ESS = (sum_i r_i)^2 / (n * sum_i r_i^2)

Fresh ratios cluster; stale replay spreads and concentrates mass.

Slide 2

P3O turns ESS into an update control

High ESS keeps the score cap loose and behavioral KL pressure low. Low ESS tightens the score cap and increases behavioral KL to damp off-policy updates.

Mechanism to show

ESS drives both capping and KL weighting, so the batch statistic changes the loss.
