Research

I am interested in working on meaningful research on natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning. My goal is to build efficient, effetive and faithful systems to tackle the great ambiguity of natural languages.

Some interesting directions I am thinking of:
(1) Developing an efficient retrieval-augmented language model (RALM) system that can handle complex real tasks;
(2) Developing constraint-following systems with language models and external augmentation for complex planning;
(3) Developing efficient and effective model evaluation with compositional data, interactive tasks, and zero-shot signals
