Xinran Zhao

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I worked on Natural Language Processing research.

At HKUST, I worked on commonsense reasoning, pronoun coreference resolution, and knowlegde graph mining with Prof. Yangqiu Song in Knowledge Computation Group. I also worked on text simplification and an online learning platform with Prof. Dit-Yan Yeung.
During my exchange at Cornell University, I started to work on debate argument mining with Prof. Claire Cardie.

I did my bachelors at HKUST with a GPA of 3.958 (top 1%) and received the Academic Achievement Medal and First Class Honor. I am actively looking for a Ph.D. position in NLP.
